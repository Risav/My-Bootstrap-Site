Our history

Starting with just a few trucks and a handful of employees, we aimed to provide reliable and affordable moving solutions to both residential and commercial clients.

With plans for further expansion and continued investment in technology and sustainability, we looks forward to shaping the future of moving services for years to come.

Our locations

'Move IT' established in the bustling city of Brisbane, gaining reputation for professionalism and efficiency in the local market with customer satisfaction.

'Move IT' Capitalized on its success and began expanding its operations beyond Chicago, gradually establishing a presence in neighboring states and cities such as Sydney and Melbourne.
